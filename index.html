Some definitions are in order

Good food: high quality animal protein, healthy fats, non-starchy vegetables, and some fruits

Bad food: anything high in carbohydrates and/or highly processed (what Michael Pollin calls foodlike substances)

The principle you need to accept for this to work

If your BMI is not somewhere in the middle of the healthy range, your diet is not good. Or in other, more candid words, the only reason you are overweight is that you eat too much bad food
